<p class="yearTEXT"><i>March 22, 2020</i></p>
<p class="TEXT">
              We are proud to launch  <b>Eterna's OpenVaccine challenge</b>. These puzzle seeks improved mRNA sequences for COVID-19 vaccines, with a special focus to stabilizing these molecules against degradation to help enable their deployment for mass immunization. Experimental tests are being carried out in collaboration with <a href="http://barnalab.stanford.edu/">Maria Barna's lab</a> in Stanford Genetics. Come join us!
