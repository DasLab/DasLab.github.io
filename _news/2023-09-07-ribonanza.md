<p class="TEXT">
              We’ve teamed up with Kaggle to accelerate progress in RNA structure prediction through a new machine learning competition. Check out the new <a href="https://www.kaggle.com/c/stanford-ribonanza-rna-folding/"><b>Ribonanza</b></a> challenge! Register by Nov. 30, 2023, deadline for model submission is Dec. 7, 2023. Winning models to be open sourced and presented at NeurIPS MLSB workshop. Visit the <a href="https://www.kaggle.com/c/stanford-ribonanza-rna-folding/">competition webpage</a>.
